New Addition

NeoPhotonics (NPTN) is a Zacks Rank #2 (Buy) and was roughed up a little yesterday in the broader tech sell off, but the stock looks to be on the road to recovery.

The company makes optoelectronic  products for communication networks that run on optical signals.  The build out of fiber networks is something that will only increase over time as the demand for greater bandwidth is more or less a constant.

The stock was trading over $9 not too long ago following a big gap up from the upper $6 range thanks to a huge beat.  Analysts loved the quarter and raised estimates, thus the higher rank, but many believe the turnaround story is a good one.

B Riley FBR upgraded the stock after the report to a buy from neutral and moved their target price on the stock from $6.50 to $10.  That is what we like to see.

Needham, the axe in the space, has a strong buy on the stock and a $9 price target.

DA Davidson has a $7.50 target price and notes that next quarter is guided to (at the mid point) growth of 14% on the top line.
